--- title: UTS Games Studio date: 2008-04-01 siteUrl: games.it.uts.edu.au teaser: Drupal configuration and deployment. teaserImage: games-studio-icon.png contentImage: games-studio-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/not-for-profit - portfoliopay/voluntary --- The UTS Games Studio is a small group of students and researchers whose interests include graphics programming, virtual reality, animation, interaction design, and game development. The studio is affiliated with the UTS Creativity and Cognition Studios, as well as the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology. While doing my Honours year in IT during 2008, I was a member of the Games Studio. I built a relatively simple Drupal site for the Games Studio, using a third-party theme with some small modifications, and using very few add-on modules. Most of the site consists of static pages managed hierarchically with the book module. The news archive and the profile directory are structured sections built with CCK and Views.
