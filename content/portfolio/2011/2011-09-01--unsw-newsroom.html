The Media and Communications Office — the unit responsible for the site — decided to rebuild the Newsroom from the ground up. The new site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was a secondary developer on this project. I was tasked in a support and bug fixing role for the Newsroom. I assisted with CSS changes, with Drupal theme changes, with module development, and with configuration / deployment.
