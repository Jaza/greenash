--- title: UNSW Grand Challenges date: 2015-08-01 siteUrl: grandchallenges.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development and deployment. teaserImage: grandchallenges-icon.jpg contentImage: grandchallenges-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- UNSW Grand Challenges is a project that was launched by the UNSW Sustainability Office. Its centrepiece is an initiative called the "Grand Climate Challenge". The Office commissioned a site to promote the project. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at Vector 5, I was the lead developer on this project. The site is a fairly basic one-page Drupal affair. It allows upcoming events to be listed, it allows embedding of videos from YouTube, and it has a mailing list subscription form.
