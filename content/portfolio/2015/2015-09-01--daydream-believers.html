--- title: Daydream Believers date: 2015-09-01 siteUrl: daydreambelievers.greenash.net.au teaser: Complete Flask site development and deployment. teaserImage: daydreambelievers-icon.jpg contentImage: daydreambelievers-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/not-for-profit - portfoliopay/voluntary --- The Daydream Believers Performers is a singing group in Sydney's east, for children with special needs, their families, and other caring souls. The group desired a web presence to promote their activities online. I volunteered to build a site for this purpose. The site is built in Flask, and is hosted by GreenAsh free of charge. The site is contained in a single page, and it includes a photo gallery, an events listing, and a contact form. All content on the site is editable by administrators. The CMS-like app that I built for this project, I later cleaned up and released as the open-source Flask Editable Site project.
