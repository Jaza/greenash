--- title: Barmy Home Page date: 1999-03-01 permalink: /portfolio/barmy-home-page/ siteUrl: jaza-barmy.greenash.net.au teaser: Complete site design and development. teaserImage: jaza-barmy-icon.jpg contentImage: jaza-barmy-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/personal --- I created this site as an online album for my Barmitzvah, which I celebrated when I was just 13 years of age. The site proudly proclaims that it was created with the evil Microsoft FrontPage, and that it is viewed best with Internet Explorer 4 — but hey, this was 1999; and hey, I was 13 years old; and hey, you've gotta start somewhere, right? Apart from the [incredibly crude] first version of Jaza's World, this was my first real attempt at designing and creating a web site; and all things considered, I think I did a reasonable job of it. The site has a number of photo galleries, as well as several pages of static text. The design is tag-soup, but it looks pretty good, and (somehow) it still renders quite well on modern browsers. The site was also recently migrated over to GreenAsh, and it's former home at Free Web Space Inc has been closed down.
