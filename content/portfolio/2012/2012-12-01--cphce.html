--- title: CPHCE date: 2012-12-01 siteUrl: www.cphce.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development and deployment. teaserImage: cphce-icon.jpg contentImage: cphce-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The UNSW Centre for Primary Health Care and Equity is a research centre that aims to address health inequalities and to improve community health care, with a focus on several locations in the Sydney area. The Centre commissioned a new web site to replace their ageing web presence. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site is a fairly standard UNSW Drupal affair: it has a news / events section; a projects listing; and a publications archive.
