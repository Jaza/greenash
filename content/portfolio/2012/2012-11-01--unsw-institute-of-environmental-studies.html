--- title: UNSW Institute of Environmental Studies date: 2012-11-01 siteUrl: www.ies.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development and deployment. teaserImage: ies-icon.jpg contentImage: ies-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The UNSW Institute of Environmental Studies is an interdisciplinary centre affiliated with the University's Faculty of Science. The Institute commissioned a new web site to replace their ageing web presence. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site is a fairly standard UNSW Drupal affair: it has a news / events section; a large front-page carousel block; and a staff / researcher profile listing in the 'our people' section.
