The School commissioned a new web site to replace their ageing web presence. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site is a fairly standard UNSW Drupal affair: it has a news / events section; a large front-page carousel block; and a staff / researcher profile listing in the 'contacts and people' section.
