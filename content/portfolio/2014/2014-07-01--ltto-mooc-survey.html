--- title: LTTO MOOC Survey date: 2014-07-01 siteUrl: www.coursera.org/course/ltto teaser: Drupal site development and custom module development. teaserImage: lttomoocform-icon.jpg contentImage: lttomoocform-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- Learning to Teach Online is an innovative MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) that launched in 2014, delivered on the Coursera platform by a group of academics from UNSW. As well as the main content on Coursera, the LTTO co-ordinators commissioned the development of a prototype custom survey as a separate site, for the MOOC's students to reflect on their experience and to receive suggestions for further learning. The survey site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time employee at Vector 5, I was the lead developer on this project. The survey site consists primarily of a series of forms, powered by the Drupal Webform module. The special feedback / suggestions pages were built as custom Drupal modules. The site's main technical feature, is its integration with Coursera using the LTI protocol, whereby students can click a link in Coursera, and be seamlessly logged in to the survey site as a third-party learning app.
