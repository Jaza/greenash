--- title: Vector 5 date: 2014-02-01 siteUrl: vector5.com.au teaser: PHP site development and templating. teaserImage: vector5-icon.jpg contentImage: vector5-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- Vector 5 is a small start-up based in Surry Hills, Sydney. I was part of the company's team from its birth at the start of 2014, first as a freelancer, and then as a full-time employee. Vector 5 specialises in custom analytics, reporting, and intelligent data solutions for a variety of clients. It's also closely affiliated with The Farm Digital, a Sydney-based digital design and branding agency. One of my first projects as part of Vector 5, was to build the company's inaugural web site. The site is a simple single-page brochureware affair, powered by a light database-free PHP back-end. The most distinctive front-end feature of the site, is its use of fullPage.js for funky, responsive, vertical and horizontal scrolling.
