--- title: Art Month Sydney date: 2010-01-01 siteUrl: artmonthsydney.com teaser: Django templating and app development. teaserImage: artmonth-icon.png contentImage: artmonth-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/not-for-profit - portfoliopay/paid --- Art Month is a month-long series of art exhibitions and other events, held in venues around Sydney. It's a non-profit organisation, sponsored by various groups (commercial, government, and other), and supported by the City of Sydney. Digital Eskimo was contracted to build the web site for Art Month 2010. The site was built in Django, and it was focused primarily around the display of events. I was the assistant developer for the site. I built a large number of the front-end templates, and I also coded parts of the back-end, including the site's subscription feature that integrates with the Campaign Monitor mailing list service.
