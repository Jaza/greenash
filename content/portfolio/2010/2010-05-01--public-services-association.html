--- title: Public Services Association date: 2010-05-01 siteUrl: psa.labor.net.au teaser: Django templating and app development. teaserImage: psa-icon.png contentImage: psa-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The Public Services Association is a union that represents employees of the NSW government, and other related personnel. Digital Eskimo was contracted by the PSA, to build a new sub-site with facilities for online membership application and online fee payment. The membership sub-site is built with Django, and as an employee of DE, I was the assistant developer for the project. I built the front-end templates for the forms, with the help of the Uni-Form framework to ensure good form accessibility and consistency. I also worked on the forms' back-end integration with the SecurePay Australian payment gateway.
