--- title: UNSW Workplace Mental Health date: 2013-08-01 siteUrl: www.wmh.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development. teaserImage: wmh-icon.png contentImage: wmh-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The UNSW Workplace Mental Health Research Program aims to develop research focused on the mental health needs of NSW front line emergency workers. The Program commissioned a web site to give itself an online presence for the first time. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site is a fairly standard UNSW Drupal affair: it has a news section; a projects listing; a publications archive; and a staff listing.
