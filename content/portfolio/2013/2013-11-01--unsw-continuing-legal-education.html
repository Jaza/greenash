--- title: UNSW Continuing Legal Education date: 2013-11-01 siteUrl: www.cle.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development with e-commerce. teaserImage: cle-icon.jpg contentImage: cle-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- UNSW Continuing Legal Education is a program run by the University's Faculty of Law, and it has the purpose of providing legal professionals seminars and accredited courses relevant to their ongoing careers. The Faculty commissioned a new web site to replace the program's ageing web presence. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site's main feature is the ability for users to sign up to courses / seminars, including the handling of online payment. The Drupal Commerce module powers most of this functionality (shopping cart, checkout system, etc). I wrote a custom Drupal Commerce payment gateway module, to handle payment using the OneStop gateway, the same gateway that the legacy CLE site used.
