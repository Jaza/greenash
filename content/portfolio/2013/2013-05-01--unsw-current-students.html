--- title: UNSW Current Students date: 2013-05-01 siteUrl: student.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development and deployment. teaserImage: currentstudents-icon.jpg contentImage: currentstudents-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The UNSW Current Students website (also known as the "Student Gateway") is a new site commissioned by the University's central administration, that aims to provide relevant links and information for all things student-related. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. The site's most distinctive feature, is that it allows direct sign-on to several University services (e.g. MyUNSW, UNSW Identity Manager), from a login form on the front page. The site also contains a large amount of hierarchical informational pages, and as such, the front-end elements (e.g. header / sidebar menus, breadcrumbs) are built to accommodate both a large quantity of pages, and a deep level of nesting (up to 8 levels).
