--- title: Connected Waters date: 2013-03-01 siteUrl: www.connectedwaters.unsw.edu.au teaser: Complete Drupal site development and deployment. teaserImage: connectedwaters-icon.jpg contentImage: connectedwaters-shot.jpg tags: - portfoliotype/business - portfoliopay/paid --- The UNSW Connected Waters Initiative is a research centre focused on groundwater investigation and education in Australia. The centre commissioned a new web site to replace their ageing web presence. The site is built with Drupal. In my capacity as a full-time contractor at UNSW, I was the lead developer on this project. Along with the typical content associated with a research centre, such as news articles and staff profiles, the Connected Waters site also boasts an impressive collection of academic and educational resources, including fact sheets, in-depth articles, and videos. Migrating this content from the legacy site into Drupal was a large task in itself.
