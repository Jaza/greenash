--- title: What If Stocks date: 2018-05-01 siteUrl: github.com/Jaza/whatifstocks teaser: Complete Flask app development and deployment. teaserImage: whatifstocks-icon.jpg contentImage: whatifstocks-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/personal - portfoliopay/voluntary --- What If Stocks is a little web app that I built, to answer the question: based on a start and end date, and a pool of stocks and historical prices, what would have been the best stocks to invest in? It's a simple Flask app, with a front-end that uses stock Bootstrap for styling and components. The main custom feature that I built, was the ability to download and import historical stock prices using the Alpha Vantage API.
