--- title: WWF-Australia date: 2005-12-01 siteUrl: wwf.org.au teaser: Custom admin interface development. teaserImage: wwf-icon.jpg contentImage: wwf-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/not-for-profit - portfoliopay/paid --- The World Wide Fund for Nature Australia (WWF-Australia) hired GreenAsh to help in developing a custom administrative interface for their home-grown CMS. The interface allows staff to easily add and update pages and media assets on the site. WWF-Australia plans to release their CMS solution as an open-source product called Yak at some point in the future. GreenAsh was also (initially) hired to develop a pilot Drupal site, to assess the suitability of Drupal as a potential replacement for WWF's home-grown CMS. However, WWF ultimately decided to stick with their own solution.
