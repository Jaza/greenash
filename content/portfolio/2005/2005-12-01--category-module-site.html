--- title: Category module site date: 2005-12-01 siteUrl: category.greenash.net.au teaser: Complete site design and development. teaserImage: catmod-icon.png contentImage: catmod-shot.png tags: - portfoliotype/personal - portfoliopay/voluntary --- I decided to make the category module web site, as a support and documentation source for the Drupal category module that I wrote. I designed and implemented the distinctive theme of the site, and developed what was probably the first ever Drupal site to be powered by the category module. I also implemented Drupal's multi-site functionality, to allow sharing of some entities (such as users) between the main site and the category module demo sub-site. I have been (and continue to be) the main content contributor for this site, although the site is open to contributions from anyone. I also, naturally, take care of the site's support and maintenance.
